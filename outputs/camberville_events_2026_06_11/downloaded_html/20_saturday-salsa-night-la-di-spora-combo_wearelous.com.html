ABOUT THE ARTIST

Led by lead vocalist and multi-instrumentalist Angel Javier Camilo, La Diáspora Combo is an intergenerational collective of musicians from Latin America and the United States. Rooted in diasporic identity and community, the ensemble brings high-energy, dance-forward performances of salsa, merengue, and bachata. Honoring the Latin American tradition of musical storytelling, La Diáspora Combo reimagines the works of artists such as Rubén Blades & Willie Colón, Juan Luis Guerra, El Gran Combo, and Oscar D’León, transforming each performance into a celebration of culture, dance, and communal spirit.
