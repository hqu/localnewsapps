The Moth: Boston StorySLAM – HOLIDAY GONE WRONG

The Moth: Boston StorySLAM – HOLIDAY GONE WRONG

Tue. Jun. 30, 2026 at 6:30 pm - 9:30 pm

Join us the fourth Tuesday of each month for the Boston Moth StorySLAM! This community-focused, open-mic storytelling competition is open to anyone can share a story on the night’s theme (changes every month).

HOLIDAY GONE WRONG: Five-minute stories about vacation woes. The moments you wished you could click your heels and be back home to the times you had to McGyver your way through. Stolen passports, lost reservations, the unsung hero that saved the day. Desert mirages, dead batteries, dangerous encounters. It was about the journey, not the destination.

Get tickets now!

6:30pm Doors Open | 7:30pm Stories Begin

*Seating is not guaranteed and is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Please be sure to arrive at least 10 minutes before the show. Admission is not guaranteed for late arrivals. All sales final.

Media Sponsors: PRX and WBUR

Arts at the Armory serves delicious locally sourced food and drink at the Performance Hall bar when doors open (usually one hour before the show).
