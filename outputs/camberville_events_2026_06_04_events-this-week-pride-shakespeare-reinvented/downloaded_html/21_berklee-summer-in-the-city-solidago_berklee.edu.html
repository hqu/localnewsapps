Solidago (Ella Wheeler) is a Maryland-born bassist and songwriter who paints life’s tapestry through music. At Berklee College of Music, she continues to nurture her craft, blending jazz, neo-soul, and pop to tell stories of love, nature, and self-discovery. Influenced by legends such as Marvin Gaye, Stevie Wonder, Michael Jackson, D’Angelo, Erykah Badu, and Corinne Bailey Rae, Solidago weaves poetic lyrics and colorful harmonies into vibrant, emotive soundscapes.

Solidago will be performing as part of Berklee’s Summer in the City 2026 concert series. View series schedule.
